Stormborn

  • Stormborn Vanilla Porte

  • Grain Bill
  • Maris Otter, 3,000g
  • Pilsner Malt, 2,000g
  • Chocolate Malt, 500g
  • Wheat Malt, 500g
  • Muscavado Sugar, 250
  • Flaked Oats, 250

  • Hops Schedule
  • Mosaic 40g 40 mins
  • Yeast
  • SafaleS04 12g
  • Directions
  • Mill grains and mash at 75c Hold for 60 minutes 67c. Ferment for 4 days then add 4 vanilla pods and ferment for a further 10 days.
