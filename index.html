Dukey’s is a small independent brewery producing a range of quality small batch homebrew style beers. Founded in Haywards Heath in 2016 we expanded to our current premises in Burgess Hill in 2019. All beers are brewed in 20L batches using only the finest quality local ingredients. We also grow our own hops on site. Come and explore the range of ales we offer including a delicious Vanilla Porter (Stormborn) an American Pale Ale (Trumps Stink) and special old English ale as a homage to Brexit (Beerexit)! For a limited time we will also be stocking our one off Covid-19 lockdown Ale (Face Cask). Join us in the ‘Dukey’s Community’ pages for beer banter and recipe suggestions!
